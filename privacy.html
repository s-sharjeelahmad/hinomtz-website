Privacy Policy

How HINOMTZ PolyChem Zone collects, uses, and safeguards your business information.

Effective: February 20, 2026 Last Updated: February 20, 2026 B2B Policy

HINOMTZ PolyChem Zone ("we", "us", or "our") is a technology-driven textile chemical company based in Karachi, Pakistan. We operate exclusively in a business-to-business (B2B) capacity. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from business contacts, how we use it, and how we protect it. By contacting us or using our website at hinomtzpolychemzone.com, you agree to the practices described below.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information solely for the purpose of responding to business inquiries and processing product quote requests. We only collect what is genuinely necessary. The information we may collect includes:

  • Business email address (e.g., provided via the contact/quote form or direct email)
  • Phone or WhatsApp number for quote follow-up and order communication
  • Company name and contact person's name or job title
  • Product inquiry details — fabric type, quantity, and intended application
  • Technical questions or specifications submitted through our contact channels

We do not collect payment card details, national ID numbers, passwords, or any sensitive personal data. All communication is conducted through industry-standard business channels (email, phone, or WhatsApp).

We collect only what is necessary to respond to your B2B inquiry — nothing more.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

Information collected from business contacts is used exclusively for legitimate B2B commercial purposes. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Respond to product inquiries, sample requests, and technical questions
  • Prepare and deliver product quotations and pricing information
  • Communicate order status, delivery details, and after-sale technical support
  • Send occasional product updates, new-launch announcements, or technical bulletins (you may opt out at any time by replying to any message)
  • Fulfil legal or regulatory obligations applicable to the chemical supply industry in Pakistan

We handle all business information with care and discretion. Your details will only be accessed by authorised personnel directly involved in serving your inquiry or order. We do not engage in automated profiling or data-driven marketing targeted at individuals.

Section 03

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your business information to any third party for commercial purposes. Your contact details and inquiry data remain within HINOMTZ PolyChem Zone and are not shared with external marketing agencies, data brokers, or advertising platforms.

The only circumstances under which information may be shared with a third party are:

  • Trusted logistics or freight partners — solely to coordinate product delivery, and only with shipping-relevant details (name, address, phone)
  • Professional advisors (e.g., legal counsel or accountants) bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Government or regulatory authorities when required by applicable Pakistani law or a valid legal process

Any third party receiving data is held to strict confidentiality standards and is prohibited from using it for any purpose beyond the specific task for which it was shared.

We never sell or commercially share your data. Your business information is yours — we simply use it to serve you.

Section 04

Data Retention & Security

Business inquiry records are retained for as long as is reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required for standard commercial record-keeping. Where data is no longer needed, we securely delete it.

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures — including access controls and encrypted communication channels — to safeguard information against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. However, please note that no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
