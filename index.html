Our Story

About HINOMTZ PolyChem Zone

HINOMTZ PolyChem Zone is a technology-driven textile chemical company established in 2021, serving Pakistan’s fast-growing industrial sector. We focus on Hybrid, Intelligent, and Nano-based organosilicone technologies — delivering superior performance at lower dosage.

Our product line spans dyeing auxiliaries, finishing agents, and specialty modifiers designed to solve real production challenges: improving hand feel, colour fastness, and processing efficiency while meeting stringent eco-compliance requirements.
